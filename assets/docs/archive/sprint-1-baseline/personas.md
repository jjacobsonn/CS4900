## User Personas

### Persona 1: Designer / Contributor (Primary Content Creator)

**Role:** Designer / Creative Contributor

**Responsibilities:**

* Upload creative assets
* Respond to feedback and revise assets
* Submit updated versions for review

**Usage Characteristics:**

* Uses both desktop and mobile devices
* Interacts with assets frequently and iteratively
* Focused on version accuracy and feedback clarity

**Key Challenges:**

* Unclear or scattered feedback
* Difficulty tracking which version is under review
* Manual version naming and history tracking

**Needs Addressed by Vellum:**

* Clear version history with a single current version
* Feedback tied directly to specific revisions
* Simple asset upload and resubmission workflow

---

### Persona 2: Creative Reviewer (Primary Decision Maker)

**Role:** Marketing Manager / Product Stakeholder

**Responsibilities:**

* Review assets and provide feedback
* Approve assets or request changes
* Ensure assets meet brand or campaign standards

**Usage Characteristics:**

* Frequently reviews assets on mobile devices
* Operates in short, time-constrained sessions
* Prioritizes speed and clarity over advanced tooling

**Key Challenges:**

* Fragmented feedback channels
* Ambiguous approval state
* Desktop-first tools that are difficult to use on mobile

**Needs Addressed by Vellum:**

* Mobile-first review experience
* Structured approval states (approved, changes requested)
* One-tap feedback and decision workflows

---

### Persona 3: Admin / Project Owner (System Governance)

**Role:** Creative Lead / System Administrator

**Responsibilities:**

* Manage users, roles, and permissions
* Oversee asset progress and approval workflows
* Maintain accountability and auditability of decisions

**Usage Characteristics:**

* Oversees multiple projects and assets
* Requires a high-level view of system activity
* Primarily uses desktop environments

**Key Challenges:**

* Lack of visibility into review bottlenecks
* Approval decisions buried in unstructured comments
* Inconsistent enforcement of review policies

**Needs Addressed by Vellum:**

* Role-based access control (RBAC)
* Centralized asset status and history
* Predictable and enforceable approval workflows
